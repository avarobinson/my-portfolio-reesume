About
As a software development intern at CureMetrix, I developed a secure ground-up website for internal tracking of algorithm results using Django, Docker, and AWS.
CureMetrix uses AI to analyze and detect breast cancer in mammograms. I worked directly with the CTO and lead software developer to design and develop a GUI used to search for and analyze the results output from the algorithm. This allows internal users to query by a variety of criteria and pull images output by the algorithm down from AWS storage in order to continue to advance their computer aided detection model.
